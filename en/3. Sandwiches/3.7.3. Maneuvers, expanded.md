Interactive check of pairs: [Maneuvers game](https://jfeldman777.github.io/man/?lang=en)

A and B met. We need to understand how comfortable they are together.
Suppose, for example, A's levels = 2345, B's levels = 1678

In total they need to play 8 games. Let's play:

A2 B1 A2 = repetition of moves, cooperation
A3 B1 = war
A4 B1 = war
A5 B6 A5 = cooperation
B1 A2 B1 = cooperation
B6 A5 B6 = cooperation
B7 A5 = war
B8 A5 = war

In total, 50% war — that is a lot.

And here is another pair

A = 2345, B = 2367

A2 B3 A2 = cooperation
A3 B2 A3 = cooperation
A4 B3 A4 = cooperation
A5 B6 A5 = cooperation
B2 A3 B2 = cooperation
B3 A4 B3 = cooperation
B6 A5 B6 = cooperation
B7 A5 = war

That is much better, but it can be better still, for example

A = 2345, B = 3456

Check that here there is no war in any variant.

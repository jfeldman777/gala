Imagine that a player of a certain level X is dropped into a territory inhabited by characters of levels from 1 to X−1.
If the player comes next to a character of level X−1, they take that character into the team. But if they alone approach a character below level X−1, they die (war).

If the player has already gathered a team — for example of levels X and X−1 — they can then join a character of level X−2, and so on.

Goals: 1) do not die 2) gather the largest possible team 3) bring it home

The difficulty of the game illustrates the problem of surviving among those who are much less intelligent than you.

Extra task — characters move according to their levels: try to describe the movement type of each level and say how that type of movement relates to the well-known general properties of characters of that level.

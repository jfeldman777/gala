Do you remember the fairy tale about the breadcrumbs?
The Brothers Grimm.
The stepmother orders her husband to take the children into the forest and leave them there for the wolves to eat. The first time, the children manage to bring along white pebbles. Dropping the pebbles along the way, they find their way back home by them. The second time, there are no pebbles left, so the children take bread and scatter breadcrumbs instead. But the crumbs get pecked up by birds. And the children stay lost in the forest.

The dark forest is my discourse, in which I wander. And to find my way home, I scatter breadcrumbs.

Go ahead and peck away, don't be shy, I have plenty!

The fourth rule says.

Every row of concepts must be justified — that is, ask of it:

Is the row homogeneous?
Is the row complete?
Is the order unique?
Where did the row come from?

Our example — the scale.
Do re mi fa sol la si do.

Is this row homogeneous? Yes

Is it complete? No

Unique order? Yes.

Origin? That is an interesting historical plot better asked of an AI. How did the modern scale (do re mi fa sol la si do) arise?

Now how to justify the justification? Why these four questions and not others?

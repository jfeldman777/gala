1. We formulate the general idea and give the project a name.
2. We determine the place and the route.
3. We determine timed checkpoints.
4. We distribute roles — who is responsible for what.
5. We appoint those responsible — who makes decisions in case of unexpected events.
6. We schedule a debrief for after the trip.

What do we keep in our field of attention at each stage?

1. An object — a name.
2. Many objects — space.
3. One line — time.
4. Many lines — distribution of roles.
5. One plane — making decisions under a shortage of time and information, but with personal responsibility.
6. Many planes. The debrief — comparing possible options and evaluating them against each other.

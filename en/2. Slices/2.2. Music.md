1. It all starts with a rattle. A point.
2. Xylophone, bells, little drums. Many points.
3. A melody. We play scales. A line.
4. Two-part harmony, many-part harmony, choir, orchestra, ensemble. Many lines.
5. Improvisation. A point of choice. A plane.

Many planes — symphonic music.

For me, music is a universal metaphor for human life.
Every person is an instrument.
Life is a live performance.

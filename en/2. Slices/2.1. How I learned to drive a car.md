1. Sat in the driver's seat. Before, I'd sat in the passenger seat. Terrifying! I remember gripping the wheel and for a few seconds seeing nothing but the wheel. One object.
2. Gradually calmed down. I hear the instructor's voice. "Pedals: brake on the left, gas on the right. Ignition key. Turn signals, wipers. Gear shifting..." Many objects.
3. And now, attention! To drive in a straight line, you need to perform the following sequence of actions:... Did it. Drove in a straight line. Got onto a single-lane highway. Driving, driving. A line.
4. Got onto a multi-lane highway. Lane on the left, lane on the right, passing. Parallel lines.
5. Approached an intersection. Which way do we turn? A plane — a window of possibilities, a point of choice.

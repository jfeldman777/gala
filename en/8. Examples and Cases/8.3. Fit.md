Suppose you have been hired. What does that mean in Levels?

That the collective has favorite levels (usually 4) and you have at least one of those four (otherwise they will not hire you).

And then?

If you share 4 with the system — you are wholly in the work, neither home nor hobbies.

If three — home and hobbies, but work is a joy.

If two — they will not fire you, but when you quit yourself they will be glad.

If 1 — they will fire you at the first opportunity and forget you like a bad dream.

Verified on myself.

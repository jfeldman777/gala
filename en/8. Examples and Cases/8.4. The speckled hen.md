This example shows how one must analyze a plot so that its levels become clear.

Once upon a time there lived a grandfather and a grandmother, and they had a speckled hen
(the cycle of life, there is property = 4)

One day the hen laid an egg — not a simple egg — a golden one
(disruption of the habitual order of things by the will of the hen — 5)

Grandfather beat it — did not break it, grandmother beat it — did not break it
(Grandfather and grandmother went mad — instead of asking the question — how best to use the new resource — 6 — they took up a mad task — beating an egg with no plan at all. 31)

A mouse ran by, flicked its tail
The egg fell and broke

The mouse adds still more chaos = 51

Grandfather cries, grandmother cries (1), and the hen clucks
Don’t cry, grandfather, don’t cry, grandmother
I will lay you a new egg — not golden, a simple one

The hen uses 2 and 4: comforts, restores order
grandfather again 3 and grandmother 34

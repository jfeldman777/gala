
Life is a continuous flow in which we all swim.
Funnels are where the flow breaks.
Situations are funnels worth noticing.
Problems are situations where we intend to improve our behavior.
Tasks are problems in which it is clear what counts as a good solution.
Questions are any of the above (except life)

Examples

Does God exist?
Why does the idea of God inevitably arise in history?

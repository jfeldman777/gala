Howard Gardner introduced the concept of Multiple Intelligences.
If intelligence is the ability to solve problems, Gardner divides all problems into 8 classes:

1) Linguistic = command of speech, writing, reading
2) Logical-mathematical = logic, mathematics, analysis
3) Spatial = working with images, maps, drawings
4) Musical = perceiving and creating music
5) Bodily-kinesthetic = control of the body, movement
6) Interpersonal = understanding other people
7) Intrapersonal = understanding oneself
8) Naturalistic = distinguishing natural objects, animals, plants

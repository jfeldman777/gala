Many professions emphasize some codings at the expense of others.
That is why close observation of a student during their school years — their success in some subjects and failure in others — is important evidence that some of their codings are more resilient than others.

The following link between a subject and a coding is fairly obvious.
Mathematics — scheme, meaning.
Literature, foreign languages — text.
Geography, history — picture, scenario.
Art — image.
Philosophy — text, meaning.
Physics — scheme.
Theoretical physicist — meaning, text.
Experimental physicist — scenario, picture.

The codings involved can change from operation to operation.
For example, a clerk receiving goods uses the hand and the eye.
Level 3 — see it through to the end; level 5 — make a quick decision if something is wrong.
Following the rules = level 4.

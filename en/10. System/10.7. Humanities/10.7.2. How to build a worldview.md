A worldview can be built by relying on Philosophy and/or the Humanities.

I have put both in order.
Now they can be relied on.

Does a worldview need God? No, it doesn't.

Then what does it need?

Answers to important questions:
	1) What are good and evil
	2) Where and why does history flow
	3) What is my place in the world, and how do I reach it

We have covered the first two questions in detail; let's take up the third.

The third question breaks down into three tasks:

1) who am I in the space of states right now — does my idea of my own capabilities match those capabilities
2) expand my capabilities as much as possible — ultimately learning to consciously shift myself into any state in a short time
3) learn to read the states of other people (as well as of tasks and communities)
4) win in any situation by choosing the optimal state
5) use this advantage so that as many people as possible take up the position described above

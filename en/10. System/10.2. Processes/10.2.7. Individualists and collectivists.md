The choleric and the sanguine run ahead of the crowd and get used to not taking others' opinions into account. This is how individualists are formed.

The phlegmatic and the melancholic run behind the majority and get used to hearing others' opinions first before adding their own. This is how collectivists are formed.

This affects the levels.

The point is that odd levels do not see other people and other opinions (one object, ...), while even levels do (many objects...).

So the choleric and the sanguine display odd levels, while the phlegmatic and the melancholic display even ones. This matters when building a personality map.

Every strategy corresponds to its own state of mind (and its own requirements). Using a strategy out of place gets punished. So people train for a particular strategy and choose their tasks accordingly.
That is why the vast majority specialize in one particular strategy. However, not everyone is satisfied with staying fixed within this set — so there are additional strategies.

![[Pasted image 20260714172158.png|697]]

The Wanderer strategy climbs from consumer up to author — and then moves into another pyramid, starting again from the bottom.

The Invader changes any strategy for any other.

The Outsider steps outside the infological fabric entirely — and outside any requirements.

The difficulty of a strategy is an objective quantity, as follows:

0 — Outsider
12 — consumer
34 — transmitter
56 — author
78 — wanderer
9 — invader

The numbers on the left are the levels (this deserves separate treatment).

Jung identified a system of archetypal roles in the psyche.

1. Child
2. Mother
3. Father
4. Hero
5. Sage
6. Trickster

In fact, these are just the first 6 levels, rearranged:

(1) Child
(2) Mother
(3) Hero
(4) Father
(5) Trickster
(6) Sage

Notice this: Freud and Jung are not directly comparable. But through the levels, they are!

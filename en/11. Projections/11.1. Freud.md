Freud's model of personality consists of three agents:

ID — dark energy from the depths of the unconscious, mostly about sex.
EGO — the will to obtain what the ID desires.
SUPER-EGO = social mechanisms that restrain the ID+EGO by means of GUILT and SHAME.

My interpretation.
There aren't three agents, there are four.
ID = level 1 (I want)
EGO = level 3 (I will get it no matter what)
SUPER-EGO = level 2 (guilt) and level 4 (shame)

And this is still an incomplete list of the social mechanisms of pressure (there is also conscience at 6, and empathy at 8).

As a child I learned two similar words:
engine
and propulsion

An engine is what converts a store of energy (for example in a gas tank) into mechanical energy.
Propulsion is what delivers that energy to the self-motion of the device.

For example, in a car it is the driveshaft and gearbox, and in a boat — the propeller, though the engine may be the same.


So what in history is the engine, and what is the propulsion?
